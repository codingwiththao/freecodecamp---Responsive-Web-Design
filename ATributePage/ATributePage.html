      May 19, 1925 - Born in Omaha, Nebraska.

      1952 - Is released from prison on parole.
      1953 - Joins the Nation of Islam and changes his surname to "X" to symbolize his lost African identity.
      1957 - Establishes the first Nation of Islam mosque in New York City.
      1964 - Leaves the Nation of Islam and embarks on a pilgrimage to Mecca.
      February 21, 1965 - Is assassinated during a speaking engagement in New York City.
